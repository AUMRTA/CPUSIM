STA NUM
(Store the input into num)

INP
(Take another input)

CMP
(Complementing the second input)

INC
(Increasing the complemented number by 1)

ADD NUM
(Adding the complemented numnber and number stored in NUM)

OUT
(Printing the number)

HLT
(Halting the program)

NUM: data 1 0
